About Us

We are a dedicated team of changemakers committed to addressing critical social and environmental challenges through sustainable solutions.

Change Makers was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to create meaningful, lasting change in communities facing the greatest challenges. What began as a small group of dedicated volunteers has grown into a global network of changemakers united by a shared commitment to social justice, environmental sustainability, and human dignity.

Our approach is rooted in the belief that sustainable change comes from within communities. We work alongside local partners, respecting cultural contexts and indigenous knowledge while providing resources, expertise, and support to amplify existing efforts.

Through collaborative partnerships, innovative programs, and a dedication to measurable outcomes, we're building a more equitable and sustainable world—one community at a time.

Our Mission

We focus on creating sustainable solutions in three critical areas to create lasting positive impact.

Education

We believe education is the cornerstone of social progress. Our programs focus on increasing access to quality education for underserved communities, with special emphasis on girls' education and digital literacy.

Environment

Our environmental initiatives focus on conservation, sustainable resource management, and community-based climate adaptation strategies that protect vulnerable ecosystems while supporting local livelihoods.

Community Development

We empower communities through microfinance initiatives, skills training, and infrastructure projects that build resilience and create opportunities for sustainable economic growth.
